Recommended Ebook Reader

Make sure you have installed the eBook Reader on your device before browsing CHiLO Book. The recommended eBook Reader for CHiLO Books is as follows.

  • iOS,Mac OS: iBooks
  • Android: Gitden EPUB3 Book Reader
  • Windows: Readium

If you use eBook readers other than the ones shown above, the videos may not play normally.

About CHiLO Book

You can study offline. However, internet connection is necessary for the online tests.

It is necessary to install an eBook Reader.

By using the features of the eBook Reader app, memos and bookmarks can be saved.
